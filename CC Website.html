Who We Are

A Highly Competent Consultancy at the Crossroads of Cultures

CC (formerly Jasmine Conseils) bridges academic research and practical project management across politics, civil society, and media. We work at the intersection of geopolitics, digital innovation, and intercultural dialogue to deliver real impact across the Euro-Mediterranean region.

From countering disinformation to empowering youth and strengthening civil society networks, our expertise spans policy analysis, cross-cultural engagement, and digital innovation across the Mediterranean, MENA, and Sahel regions.
